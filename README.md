# 🦈 Mar Sustentable — Shark Sighting Reporter

> A citizen science mobile app empowering divers and locals to report shark sightings around Cozumel Island — with on-chain rewards for every verified contribution.

Built at **EthGlobal Cannes** by [Mar Sustentable](https://marsustentable.org) · Developed for marine biologist **Nadia Rubio**.

---

## What it does

Mar Sustentable turns every ocean-goer into a field researcher. Users submit georeferenced shark sightings — including photos, GPS coordinates, species, and behavior — directly from their phones. Each validated report is stored immutably and rewards the contributor with **SHARK tokens** (ERC-20), creating a sustainable incentive loop for open marine data collection.

Nadia and her team access a live dashboard showing all sightings on a real-time map, giving Mar Sustentable a continuously growing, community-powered dataset for conservation research around Cozumel.

---

## About Mar Sustentable

[Mar Sustentable](https://marsustentable.org) is a marine conservation organization based in Cozumel, Mexico. Founded by biologist Nadia Rubio, it focuses on protecting shark populations and reef ecosystems in the Caribbean through science, education, and community engagement.
